Davis, Michael C. age 67, passed away at home on October 23, 2020. Michael was born in Kalamazoo, Michigan on February 21, 1953, the son of the late Charles N. and Marian L. (VanWoerkom) Davis. He was a graduate of Comstock High School. Michael was employed at J.A. Richards Company for 31 years and owner of Davis Steel Rule Die, LLC for 19 years. He enjoyed working on and restoring cars and was a dedicated volunteer for Boy Scouts of America during the years his son, Matthew, was a scout. On October 12, 1974, Michael was united in marriage to the former Barbara E. Cline, who survives. He is also survived by his children, Matthew M. (Sarah) Davis and Ashley E. (Jeremy Keller) Davis; grandchildren, Emma, Sophia and Magnolia Davis and Noah and Anna Keller; brother, Douglas J. (Vicki) Davis and several aunts, uncles, nieces, nephews and cousins. No services are scheduled. Memorial contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society.
